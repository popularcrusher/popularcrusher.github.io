PE Series Jaw Crusher

PE jaw crusher has simple structure, reliable operation, convenient maintenance, it is widely used in many fields of mining, smelting, building materials, highway, railway, water conservancy and chemical industries. According to many years of experience and technology development, jaw crusher can meet the requirements of the primary and secondary crushing most devastating of two. Jaw crusher row open and flexible machine settings allow perfect equipment. Our jaw crusher is easy to install, operate and maintain. In accordance with feedback, our experts will make our products continue to improve to meet the changing needs of customers.

Working Principle of jaw crusher

Motor driven belt and pulley, through the eccentric shaft, to move up and down movement of the jaws to move bracket and jaw angle becomes larger, so as to promote dynamic between jaw jaw rises close to the fixed jaw plate, while the material is being squeezed, rubbing, grinding and other multiple broken; when the movable jaw down, elbow board and dynamic jaw angle is small, action under the rod moving jaw plate left, spring fixed jaw plate and crushed material from the crushing cavity outlet, with the motor moving jaw of continuous rotary crusher for crushing and discharging cycle, to realize batch production.

Features of jaw crusher

  1. The application of world-class manufacturing processes and high-tech materials;
  2. Integrated steel structures are used for the base of bearing;
  3. The crushing chamber uses "V"-shaped structure, so that the feeding size equal to the nominal one;
  4. The crushing chamber is deep and without dead zone, increases the capacity and the output;
  5. Lubrication system is safe and reliable, convenient to replace parts, easy to maintain;
  6. The outlet employs shim adjustment system, convenient and reliable, and increase the flexibility of the equipment;
  7. Low noise, less dust.
