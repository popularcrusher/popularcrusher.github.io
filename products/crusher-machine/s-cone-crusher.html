S Series Cone Crusher

For higher productivity, better reliability and flexibility, longer service life, and better final product quality, the S series cone crusher is your best choice absolutely. S series cone crusher offers several outstanding features such as excellent tramp release system, unique hydraulic lifting system, all of these features ensure the S series cone crusher offer the unbeatable performance in secondary, tertiary and quaternary applications. The innovations like stable lubrication system, excellent sealing feature, evidently reduce the production cost of material processing per ton. All of these can help you to achieve the highest level of profitability.

Working Principle Of S Series Cone Crusher

The working principle of S cone crusher is very complex. When the spring cone crusher is at work, the eccentric shaft is rotated through a horizontal axis and a pair of bevel gears. The crushing cone shaft is forced to swing by the eccentric sleeve. The broken wall surface to or from the rolling mortar wall now and make in the crushing cavity in the ore through continuous extrusion crushing and bending fracture left. This is a simple introduction to the work principle.
