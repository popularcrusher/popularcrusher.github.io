BWZ Series Heavy Duty Apron Feeder

According to customers’ feedback and an in-depth study of various transmissions, Zenith developed the new feeding machine, BWZ series heavy duty apron feeder. It is the achievement of Zenith decades-years experience in manufacturing and the world-class advanced technology. BWZ series heavy duty apron feeder is especially suitable for short-distance transmission and complex environment.

Features

  1. It has high tension;
  2. The track ensures its stable performance;
  3. Excellent adaptability, the temperature, moisture and the environment have little or no effect on it;
  4. Easy to operate, long service life and easy to maintain.
