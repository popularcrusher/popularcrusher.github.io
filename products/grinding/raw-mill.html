Raw Mill

Raw mill is mainly used in grinding raw materials and end products in cement plant. It is also suitable for various ores and other materials metallurgy, mine, chemical and construction. Besides, it has two forms of milling, one is an open form, and the other is a recycle close form equipped with powder concentrator. It is widely applied in many industries and has convenient operation. Zenith's raw mill also have small ones, which offer an ideal choice for those cement producers who seek cement raw mill for rent or sale.

Features

  1. Strong adaptability;
  2. Continuous production;
  3. Large crushing ration;
  4. Easy to adjust the output product's size.
