Vertical Roller Mill

Vertical roller mill has developed very rapidly and play a role is becoming more and more important, in the society, can meet the requirements of the industry, but also to promote the development of vertical roller mill industry, Shanghai West Zhi mining machinery as a famous vertical roller mill business will strive to develop vertical roller mill, vertical roller mill will do better, promote the development of China's economy. The vertical mill and the latest technology and rich experience from Taiwan and Germany are the latest technology and rich experience in the development of the vertical roller mill, which has been praised by customers. It integrates crushing, drying, grading, transportation as one, and taking a small space.

Working Principle Of LVertical Roller Mill

Through the drive plate of the drive plate rotating machine through the center of the air into the wind turbine disk, and hot air from the entrance to the mill. As the disk rotates, the material in the action of the centrifugal force, the edge of the plate to move, through an annular groove rolling and grinding disc and disc broken edge of materials and high-speed air flow from the air ring, large particles directly into the mill and separator material flow through, according to the influence of the rotation of the rotor and dietary barrels from cone disk fell heavy grinding, qualified powder and air and the grinding wheel dust collecting device of the collection, containing dried, by regulating air temperature and moisture content of materials, to meet the different needs, in order to achieve the desired material products of hot water and air contact. By adjusting the separator, the thickness of the product can be realized.

Features of vertical roller mill

  1. It is quite environmentally friendly, LUM is of little dust and noise;
  2. The final products has little iron and the whiteness and purity are quite high;
  3. LUM has excellent grinding-efficiency for the special design of roller shell and grinding plate curve;
  4. LUM use PLC/DCS automatic control system, well save the cost of operators.
