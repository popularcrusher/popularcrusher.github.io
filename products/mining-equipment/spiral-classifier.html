FL Spiral Classifier

Zenith FL spiral classifiers, also called screw classifier, are widely used for separation plant They can be combined with ball mill to form a closed circuit for preliminary classification and check classification,sometimes they can be applied for ore washing, desliming and dewatering as well.According to the principle that different grains are with different specific gravity and sedimentation rate in the liquid, the fine ore flows in the water and the coarse ore sinks in the bottom.

Features

  1. Simple structure and easy to maintain;
  2. Easy operation and stable performance;
  3. Gravity connection with Ball mills,slurry pump conveying is not necessary;
  4. Large volume with buffering and conveying effect, better for grinding work.
