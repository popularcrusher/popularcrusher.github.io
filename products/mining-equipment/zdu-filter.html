ZDU Series Belt Vacuum filter

ZDU series belt vacuum filter is a high-efficiency beneficiation equipment. On the basis of introducing foreign technology of the same kind products,Rubber Belt Vacuum filter is a kind of highly efficient solid-liquid separating equipment through our research. ZDU Series Belt Vacuum filter has been widely used in sectors such as metallurgy,mining,chemical engineering,paper making, food, pharmacy and environment protection.

Features

  1. It in fact has realized continuous filtering, cake cleaning,residue unloading and filter fabrics cleaning;
  2. The control system is taking DCS technology. It can realised remote automatic control;
  3. It is flexible to assemble equipment because of the mudular structure and convenient for the transportation and installation according to the field to adjust the Length and width;
  4. Higher productive efficiency, lower power consumption;
  5. The filter colth and belts can automatic tension and correct the deviation;
  6. Rubber belt edge adopts undulation skirt-brim bonded on the parallel rubber belt, serving lift can be longer.
