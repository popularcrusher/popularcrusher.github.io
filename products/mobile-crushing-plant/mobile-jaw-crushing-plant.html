Y Series Mobile Jaw Crushing Plant

Y Series Mobile Jaw Crushing Plant,also called mobile jaw crusher, which is fully adaptable for all mobile crushing needs, and it sets up a new range of business opportunities for contractors, quarry operators, recycling and mining applications. It can eliminate the obstacles of the crushing places and circumstances in open-pit mining quarries.

Zenith's mobile jaw crusher has a wide recycling and mining application. It offers high efficient and low cost project plan without environment limit for the client.

Features

  1. Incorporated design;
  2. Truck mounted;
  3. Quick road transportation;
  4. With support legs on truck, quick to set-up.
